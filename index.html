Prediction Model

We use satellite imaging data from the European Space Agency, feed it into our algorithm, and we can help reforestation efforts around the globe. We analyse, plan, orchestrate, and then monitor these reforestation efforts. Not only that, but regulators can use our service to monitor and prevent illegal deforestation.

The algorithm visually detects specific spectral bands in the electromagnetic spectrum which suggests a lack of trees. This is fed into the machine learning model which uses jacobian vectors as a way to detect areas that have emitted radiation in the same electromagnetic band which can be the warning signs of deforestation.
